Story

The story follows former captain of the second Ultramarine company Titus. After having served for 100 years in the Deathwatch. After a fatal wound he was reborn as a Primaris Spacemarine and Ready to exact vengeance against the enemies of the Imperium of Man. He must fight against Tyranids to save Imperium Planets from certain doom. However, in the shadows another foe is waiting who has been maipulating the threads of fate.
